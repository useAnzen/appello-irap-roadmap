1 · Objective

This is the Jun continuation of the NH1 codebase-tour milestone (C1-0020 covered May). Surface is the same: agentc2/CLAUDE.md, agentc2/DEPLOY.md, appello-sdlc/README.md. Goal: by end of June, NH1 has solo-merged ≥ 3 P2 hamburgers and is paired-ready to take a P1 in July.

2 · Acceptance Criteria

  • Week 1: ≥ 1 P2 hamburger solo through the factory (NH1 as cursor-launch operator).
  • Week 2: ≥ 1 P2 hamburger involving a Prisma migration (uses C1-0041 CLI).
  • Week 3: ≥ 1 P2 hamburger touching the dashboard UI (uses C1-0005 + C1-0042 components).
  • Week 4: paired with Ian on a P1 hamburger; NH1 leads, Ian observes.
  • Onboarding doc agentc2/docs/onboarding/NH1-week-5-to-8.md covers expectations + escalation rules.
  • Buddy check-ins: Ian 30 min / Mon / Wed / Fri for the month; reduces to weekly after.
  • Sign-off criteria: NH1 has +1 reviewer privilege on appello-sdlc PRs by end of Jun.

4 · Implementation Phases (30h)

  1. Hamburger selection from C1-0017 slate (4h).
  2. Week 1-4 execution + buddy check-ins (20h).
  3. Doc + sign-off (4h).
  4. Reviewer-privilege grant + announce (2h).

5 · Risks & Mitigations

  • NH1 ramp slower than 1 P2/week. Mitigation: halve P2 size; extend buddy cadence.
  • Factory outage blocks week-1 P2. Mitigation: backup hamburger pre-selected.
  • Reviewer privilege premature. Mitigation: first 5 NH1-reviewed PRs require Ian second-approval.
