1 · Objective

Same harness as C3-0232; extends the Playwright spec corpus to cover the next batch of customer-critical workflows: safety forms (JHA, toolbox talk, incident report), scheduling (crew assignment, equipment dispatch, conflict resolution), reporting (timesheet export, work-order PDF, materials usage), and material management (PO create, receiving, consumption tracking).

2 · Acceptance Criteria

  • 12 additional spec files at appello/tests-e2e/flows/: 3 safety, 3 scheduling, 3 reporting, 3 materials.
  • Same conventions as C3-0232: auth fixture, per-tenant seed, story tags, baseline trace, JSON metrics.
  • Coverage of the offline path for the 3 specs most-used in low-connectivity sites (toolbox-talk, materials receiving, equipment dispatch).
  • Reporting specs cover PDF generation quality (no overflow, all required fields rendered).
  • Material specs verify quantity math: receive 100, consume 30, on-hand = 70.
  • Flake-rate < 1% over 10 nightly runs.
  • Spec count = 27 total (15 from C3-0232 + 12 here) — exceeds the design target for Spider coverage.
  • Per-flow seed-data documented in appello/tests-e2e/flows/README.md.

5 · Risks & Mitigations

  • PDF assertions brittle. Mitigation: assert on extracted text, not pixels.
  • Conflict-resolution scenarios stateful. Mitigation: per-spec seed creates the precondition; isolated tenant.
  • Offline emulation Playwright-specific. Mitigation: dedicated context.setOffline(true) helper.
